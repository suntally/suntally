Why this exists

The bill is designed to hide the answer.

Solar offsets the generation part of your electricity — but you still pay delivery and fixed fees no matter what. Net-metering terms change, rates inflate, and the panels degrade. So "is it paying off?" is genuinely hard to see. SunTally pulls the real signal out of the noise.

How it works

Two ways in.

① Estimate a system

Considering solar, or checking a quote? Enter system cost, financing, your rates, and usage — or just drop in your proposal PDF and we'll read the numbers for you (in your browser). Get a 25-year projection: payback year, lifetime net, and where every future dollar goes.

② Analyze your ROI

Already have solar? Drop in your utility bills (PDF) or a CSV/Excel export. They're read into a local structured ledger you can edit, and the real dashboard builds itself — savings, coverage, and your effective rate over time.

1

Bring your data

Drop in your utility-bill PDFs and we'll read the numbers, import a CSV/Excel export, or paste rows by hand from the template — produced / delivered / sent-back kWh and charges.

2

It builds a local ledger

Everything is normalized into a clean structured dataset that lives in your browser. Edit any cell; export to JSON or CSV anytime.

3

See the truth

Cumulative payback, monthly savings, generation-vs-delivery split, effective rate, and seasonal coverage — the same analysis a spreadsheet nerd would build, instantly.

🔒 Your bills never leave your browser.

There is no backend. No account. No analytics on your data. Files are parsed locally and stored in your browser's own storage. It's open source — read every line and confirm it yourself.
